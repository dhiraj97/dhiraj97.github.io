About Talent Scout

Talent Scout is a web application that allows music lovers to constantly be updated with the songs and artists they love the most. We are changing the way how people find and play the music. Talent Scout is not just a “music streaming website” but it an innovative way of connecting music lovers with their preference of music and artists. Users can easily browse through popular streaming music, radio and other hosted internet sites. ‘My Streams’ provides recommendation based on your tastes and what’s trending around you. Also, users can connect with their existing sources of music like Google Music/ iTunes and Spotify. ‘My Shouts’ allows the users to share their music experience with their friends on their social media accounts (Facebook/ Twitter/ YouTube). Users can tag their friends using ‘My Shouts’and they can select their favourite artists and follow them on their social media profiles in order to be alerted to news, concerts and upcoming releases. All of this can be accessible from ‘My Artists’.
